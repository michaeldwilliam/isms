Purpose (A.5.29 / A.5.30)
Information security continuity is planned, implemented, maintained and tested, and Information and Communications Technology (ICT) services are prepared for disruption. This analysis identifies each critical business process, what it depends on, and how the impact of an outage grows over time (shown at one hour, eight hours, twenty-four hours, and one week). From that escalation the organisation sets a recovery time objective (RTO — how quickly the process must be restored) and a recovery point objective (RPO — how much recent data loss is tolerable), which together drive the continuity and backup arrangements.
