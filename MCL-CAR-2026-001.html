CAR-001
A.8.2
Major Nonconformity (NC)
Two database administrators share a single privileged account on the legacy production PostgreSQL instance, eliminating individual accountability for privileged actions. Identified in the March 2026 penetration test (finding 1) and confirmed during Identity and Access Management (IAM) review.Legacy database provisioned before the individual-account standard was adopted; no subsequent remediation scheduled.Migrate to individual least-privilege Database Administrator (DBA) accounts; disable the shared account; enable per-user audit logging and Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) on database administration.
