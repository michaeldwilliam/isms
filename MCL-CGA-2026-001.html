How this analysis works
Clause 7.2(a) requires the organisation to determine the competence necessary for people whose work affects the information security management system. For each role this analysis states the competency required, the level currently held, and the resulting gap. The level scale runs None, Awareness, Working, Proficient, Expert. A gap is marked Critical (shown with a star) when a Proficient or higher requirement is not yet met, since those are the people whose competence most affects the Information Security Management System (ISMS). Each open gap carries an action to close it, which is then delivered and evidenced in the Competence and Awareness Record. This document shows the need was worked out; that record shows it was met.
