How this record maps to the standard
This single register evidences two related but distinct requirements. The Function column classifies each row from its type. Competence (Cl.7.2) covers role-specific capability for people whose work affects the Information Security Management System (ISMS), such as certifications, role-specific training, external courses, and refreshers. Awareness (Cl.7.3 / A.6.3) covers organisation-wide understanding of the information security policy and each person's part in it, such as awareness sessions, inductions, and general mandatory training. Keeping both in one record gives a single source of truth while letting an auditor read each requirement separately.
