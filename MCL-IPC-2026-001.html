Clause 4.2 requires the organisation to determine the parties relevant to its information security management system and their relevant needs and expectations. This register records each party, whether they are internal or external, what they need from the Information Security Management System (ISMS), and how the Information Security Management System (ISMS) addresses it. The right-hand column points to where each need is met.
