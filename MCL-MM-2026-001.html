1. Monitoring and measurement approach (Cl.9.1)
This document records how Monarch Consolidated Ltd monitors and measures the performance and effectiveness of its Information Security Management System (ISMS), as required by Clause 9.1. Each metric below has a defined method, frequency and owner. Results are recorded over time and evaluated against target. Shortfalls are raised at the management review and, where needed, drive a corrective action. The set of metrics is reviewed at least annually.
