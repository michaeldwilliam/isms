Information Security Policy
Monarch Consolidated Ltd · ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Cl. 5.2
Owner: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) · Version: 1.0 (Draft) · · Audit-ready · Review: Every 6 months
Abbreviations used in this document
APIApplication Programming Interface
ICTInformation and Communications Technology
1. Purpose
This policy sets out Monarch Consolidated Ltd's commitment to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of its information assets. It establishes the framework within which all other information security policies operate.
2. Scope
This policy applies to Payment processing platform, customer financial data, third-party Application Programming Interface (API) integrations, and all staff. and to all personnel, contractors, and third parties acting on behalf of Monarch Consolidated Ltd.
3. Policy statements
  • Top Management commits to establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving an Information Security Management System (ISMS) aligned to ISO/IEC 27001:2022.
  • Information security objectives shall be established, measured, and reviewed at planned intervals.
  • All personnel are responsible for complying with this policy and reporting suspected security events.
  • Adequate resources shall be allocated to information security.
4. Roles and responsibilities
  • Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) — owns and approves this policy.
  • Information security function — maintains and monitors compliance.
  • All personnel — comply with this policy and report breaches.
5. Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy shall be requested in writing, risk-assessed, approved by Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), recorded, and reviewed at expiry.
6. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action. Compliance shall be monitored by the information security function and reported to Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).
7. Review
This policy shall be reviewed on a every 6 months basis, or following significant change, by Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).

Access Control Policy
Monarch Consolidated Ltd · ISO/IEC 27001:2022 A.5.15–A.5.18
Owner: Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) · Version: 1.0 (Draft) · · Audit-ready · Review: Every 6 months
1. Purpose
This policy defines how access to Monarch Consolidated Ltd's information and systems is granted, reviewed, and revoked, ensuring access is limited to authorised individuals on a need-to-know basis.
2. Scope
This policy applies to all access to Payment processing platform, customer financial data, third-party Application Programming Interface (API) integrations, and all staff., whether logical or physical.
3. Policy statements
  • Access shall be granted on the principles of least privilege and need-to-know.
  • Each user shall have a unique identity; shared accounts are prohibited except where formally authorised and compensating controls exist.
  • Access rights shall be reviewed at planned intervals and revoked promptly on role change or termination.
  • Privileged access shall be restricted, individually assigned, and logged.
4. Roles and responsibilities
  • Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) — owns and approves this policy.
  • Information security function — maintains and monitors compliance.
  • All personnel — comply with this policy and report breaches.
5. Exceptions
Exceptions to this policy shall be requested in writing, risk-assessed, approved by Chief Information Security Officer (CISO), recorded, and reviewed at expiry.
6. Compliance
Non-compliance with this policy may result in disciplinary action. Compliance shall be monitored by the information security function and reported to Chief Information Security Officer (CISO).
7. Review
