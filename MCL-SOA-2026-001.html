Cl.6.1.3 note: This Statement of Applicability records the organisation's decisions on which ISO/IEC 27001:2022 Annex A controls are applicable to the Information Security Management System (ISMS) scope. Controls marked Not Applicable must have a documented justification. All exclusions are subject to review by the certification body during Stage 1 audit. Controls marked Applicable must be addressed through the risk treatment plan. This document must be maintained and updated as the Information Security Management System (ISMS) matures.
