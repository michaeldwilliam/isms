About this engagement

Monarch Consolidated Ltd is a fictional FCA-regulated payments provider used as the basis for this portfolio engagement. The scenario places the organisation at pre-Stage 2 certification: a mature ISMS with three P1 blockers requiring remediation before audit.

The five deliverables were produced using the same workflow and toolkit applied to real engagements, demonstrating end-to-end method rather than isolated outputs. Findings thread coherently from gap analysis through risk treatment to the final engagement report.
